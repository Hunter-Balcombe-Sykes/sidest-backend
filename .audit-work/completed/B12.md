# B12 — Image MIME sniff

## Summary

Added `finfo(FILEINFO_MIME_TYPE)` byte-level MIME verification before any GD decode or R2 storage in the image pipeline. Two sites hardened:

1. **#V5-015** `ImageVariantService::loadImage()` — finfo sniff before `getimagesize()`. Throws `UnprocessableImageException` on mismatch (no-retry path in the job). Added `ALLOWED_IMAGE_MIMES` constant.

2. **#V5-047** `BrandDesignMediaService` — `assertMimeAllowed()` helper called in `upsertLogoFromUploadedFile()`, `upsertLogoFromBytes()`, and `addPlaceholder()`. `buffer()` variant used for Shopify-CDN-fetched bytes; `file()` variant for UploadedFile paths.

Also updated `SyncShopifyBrandDesignJobTest` to serve a structurally valid 1×1 PNG (the old `"\x89PNG\r\n\x1a\nfakebytes"` lacks an IHDR chunk and isn't recognized as `image/png` by libmagic).
